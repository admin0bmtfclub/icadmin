Our Story

About Us

The story behind BMTFSC Innovation Club

The BMTFSC Innovation Club was founded with a singular vision: to create a thriving culture of innovation and technological excellence at Bangladesh Machine Tools Factory School And College. We believe every student has the potential to be an innovator.

Since our founding, we have been committed to providing students with the resources, mentorship, and platform they need to transform their ideas into reality. From robotics to software development, from science fairs to national competitions — we do it all.

Our club operates under the motto "Committed to Innovate" — a promise we make to every member, every day. We strive to push boundaries, challenge assumptions, and inspire the next generation of Bangladeshi innovators.
